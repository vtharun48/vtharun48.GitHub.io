About Global Trade Data

Founded in 2005, Global Trade Data has established itself as a premier provider of comprehensive import and export data solutions. Our company specializes in collecting, analyzing, and delivering accurate trade information from over 100 countries worldwide, helping businesses navigate the complex landscape of international commerce with confidence.

At Global Trade Data, we understand that timely and reliable information is crucial for making strategic business decisions in today's fast-paced global market. Our extensive database covers millions of shipment records, offering detailed insights into trade patterns, market trends, and competitive landscapes across various industries. This wealth of information enables our clients to identify new business opportunities, evaluate potential partners, monitor competitors, and optimize their supply chains.

Our team of expert analysts and researchers work tirelessly to ensure that the data we provide is not only accurate and up-to-date but also easy to understand and actionable. We employ advanced technologies and methodologies to process vast amounts of customs data, transforming it into valuable intelligence that drives business growth and innovation for our clients.

Global Trade Data serves a diverse clientele, from small businesses looking to expand their international presence to multinational corporations seeking to strengthen their global market position. Our customized solutions are designed to meet the specific needs of each client, providing them with the tools and insights necessary to thrive in an increasingly interconnected world economy.

Our Mission

To empower businesses with accurate, comprehensive, and actionable trade data that enables informed decision-making and drives international growth and success.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted source of global trade intelligence, setting the standard for excellence in the industry and contributing to a more transparent and efficient international trading system.
